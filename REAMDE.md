## 🧭 What is this?

This repository collects my solutions to the **[roadmap.sh DevOps challenges](https://roadmap.sh/devops)** — practical exercises that cover the full DevOps skill tree, starting from the Linux and scripting fundamentals all the way up to containers, CI/CD, and cloud infrastructure.

Every script here is written to be **clean, general-purpose, and reusable** — not just a one-off answer, but something worth keeping.

---

## 🐧 My Linux Journey

I started from Linux system administration and homelabbing, building hands-on experience with:

- Bare-metal and virtualized Linux environments (Proxmox, LXC)
- Shell scripting and automation
- Networking, firewalls, and self-hosted services
- Containerization with Docker and Kubernetes (K3s)
- Configuration management with Ansible

This repo is the **scripting foundation** of that journey — going back to basics, sharpening fundamentals, and documenting every step publicly.
